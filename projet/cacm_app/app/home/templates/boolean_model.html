the rules of queries based on the boolean model
- A query is a set of words with Boolean operators: AND (∧), OR (∨), NOT (¬)
- terms must be lowercase and operators must be in [+, *, -]:
  • AND : +
  • OR : *
  • NOT : -
Example :
  • t1 ∧ (t2 ∨ ¬ t3) ==> t1 AND (t2 OR NOT t3)
  • ¬ t9 ∨ ¬(t4 ∧ t3) ==> NOT t9 OR NOT(t4 AND t3)
  • t3 ∧ t12 ∧ t33 ==> t3 AND t12 AND t33
- Exact match based on the presence or absence of the query terms in the documents
Example :
  • RSV(q,d)=1 or 0
